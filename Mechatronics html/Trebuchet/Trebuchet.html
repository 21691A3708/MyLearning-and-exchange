CHAPTER 4 – TREBUCHET

Laya: Rob, this drive seems too long, and I am getting bored now. Why don't you sing for us?

Rob: I could, but do you really think I have great voice?

Laya: Rob just sing!

Rob: Okay, okay, here I go: "Humpty Dumpty sat on the wall / Humpty Dumpty had a great fall..."

Kit: Rob, you could think of no other song?!

Rob: "All the king's horses and all the king's men/Couldn't put Humpty together again!" Hahahahaha.

Laya: Very funny, Rob. Anyway, aren't you both hungry? I see endless forests on the other side of the valley. Rob, what kind of fruit would we find there?

Kit: Now that you mentioned it Laya, my stomach is rumbling. Rob, do you think I'll find some strawberries in the forest?

Rob: Hold on, kids! Kit, strawberries grow in bushes, not trees. Let's get there and see which fruits we find, maybe they could be apples.

Laya: Kit, think of a way to get us there nowww.

Kit: Hey hey, I am ready with the plan. Do you want to hear it?

Rob: Yes yes, tell us.

Kit: We can make a machine that can fly Rob to the other side.

Laya: Fly? Are you serious? Do you want to hurt Rob?

Rob: Don't you worry Laya, flying sounds exciting. I won't get hurt, I have a metal body, and only rust can hurt me.

Kit: Okay then, let's get to work, Laya. Rob, get our BLIX set please.

Laya: Kit, do you consider your best friend?

Kit: Of course he is my best friend. Why do you ask?

Laya: What is your plan to get Rob back here once he reaches the other side?

Kit: Oh no! I didn't think about that.

Laya: That's why you need me. So, listen, here's the plan. We will tie him with rope, and when Rob sits on the machine, we can pull him back once he's done picking the fruits.

Kit: As usual, genius, Laya!

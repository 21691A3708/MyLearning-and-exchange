CHAPTER 5 - SIGN BOARD

Rob: *waves his hand frantically, mouthing something*

Laya: Kit, we can't hear Rob at all! He's trying to communicate with us. How do we talk to him when he is this far away?

Kit: Remember you kept a walkie-talkie in your bag? We can just match it to Rob's controller's frequency and we should be able to contact him.

Laya: Ooh yes! I'm so glad I kept it now. Okay, here it is, and it is set. Rob! Can you hear us? (voice coming from the walkie-talkie)

Rob: Hi Laya, can you hear me?

Kit: Yes, Rob we can hear you. Phew! Are you doing okay? What fruits do you see?

Rob: Laya, Kit. Can you hear me? I can't hear you...

Kit: Laya, I think there is a problem; Rob is not able to hear us, I'm getting worried. (voice coming from the walkie-talkie)

Rob: I have bad news my friends, my controller is broken. I won't be able to hear you. Why don't you both make sign boards so that we can communicate?

Laya: Kit, quickly bring out our Blix pieces, we need to make the signs for Rob. (voice coming from the walkie-talkie)

Rob: There are lots of berries here-strawberries, and blueberries. There are even apples. I'm sure Kit will be delighted.

Kit: Wow! See Laya, this is why Rob is my best friend, he understands my needs perfectly. Mmmm, I'm so excited about the berries.

Laya: Shhhhhh. Let me listen to what Rob is speaking. (voice coming from the walkie talkie)

Rob: Laya, Kit on the sign boards, show a triangle if you want strawberries, rectangle for blueberries and square for apples, and I'll get you both enough to fill your hungry tummies.

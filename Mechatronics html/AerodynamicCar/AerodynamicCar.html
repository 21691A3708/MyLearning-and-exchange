CHAPTER 2 - AERODYNAMIC CAR

Laya: Kit, we've started our journey, but walking on the highway will take far too long.

Kit: Let's build a car that will help us get there faster, Laya. The drive will be so beautiful! Look at the mountains and the trees, I'm already excited. But what kind of a car should we build?

Laya: That's a fantastic idea, Kit. Hey Rob, what kind of car do you think we should bund?

Rob: Hmm, my calculations say that the journey is long but the roads are very good!

Laya: I already know that Mr. Smarty Pants.

Laya: This journey will take us time. Good thing our car is light and aerodynamic. It gives us speed and uses very little fuel.

Kit: Laya, what does aerodynamics mean?

Laya: When we run, we can feel the air gushing past us. It doesn't matter so much at that speed, but the faster we go, the air around us will resist the motion further. So, sports cars, fast trains and aeroplanes are designed in a way to cut through the air, so as to create least resistance. Aerodynamics is how objects move through air. Remember this the next time you see a race car.
